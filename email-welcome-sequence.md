## EMAIL 3 — First Power Tip
**Subject:** The prompt most people never use (but should)
**Send:** 4 days after Email 2
**Preview text:** One line that changes how AI thinks before it answers.

---

Hey,

Quick one this time — a single prompt tip that makes a noticeable difference.

Most people type a question and send it. The AI gives back the first answer it generates, which is often decent but rarely its best thinking.

Here's what to add to any request that matters:

> *"Before you answer, take a deep breath and work through this step by step. Show me your reasoning, then give me your final answer."*

That's it.

What changes: instead of jumping straight to an output, the AI actually works through the problem. You see the logic. You can spot if it's gone sideways before you act on the result. And the final answer is almost always sharper.

I use this for anything I'm going to act on — pricing decisions, proposal drafts, content strategy, anything where a wrong answer costs time or money.

Try it on the next thing you ask. You'll notice the difference.
