Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

This is a plain-English privacy policy. We're not hiding anything. If something isn't clear, email info@runonai.io.

Who we are

Run On AI is a content and education website at runonai.io. We produce practical AI content for small businesses, trades, and service providers. This policy covers what we collect when you use this site.

What we collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Email address — if you sign up for the newsletter or download the free prompt pack. We use Beehiiv to manage this. You can unsubscribe any time using the link in any email we send.
  • Analytics data — we use Google Analytics (or a privacy-respecting alternative) to understand how people find and use the site. This includes page views, device type, and general location (city-level). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.
  • Server logs — our hosting provider (Netlify) automatically logs IP addresses and request metadata. These are retained per their standard policy and are not used for any marketing or profiling purpose.

What we don't collect

  • We don't sell your data. Ever.
  • We don't share your email with third parties for marketing.
  • We don't run advertising on this site — no ad networks, no tracking pixels for retargeting.
  • We don't use cookies beyond what's needed for analytics and basic site function.

Email communications

If you sign up for our email list, you'll receive the welcome sequence (3 emails) and occasional updates when we publish something worth reading. We don't send daily emails, spam, or promotional emails for third-party products.

To unsubscribe: click the unsubscribe link in any email. You'll be removed immediately.

Third-party services

We use the following services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Beehiiv — email newsletter management (beehiiv.com/privacy)
  • Netlify — website hosting (netlify.com/privacy)
  • Google Analytics — site analytics (policies.google.com/privacy)

Your rights (Australia — Privacy Act)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email info@runonai.io. We'll respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top and, if appropriate, notify email subscribers. The current version will always be at runonai.io/privacy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email info@runonai.io.
