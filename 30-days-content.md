# Run On AI — 30 Days of Social Content

**Tone:** Warm, direct, Australian. Real stories from a real business.
No generic AI hype. No chest-beating. Just specific, useful, honest.

**Format guide:**
- TikTok/Reels: Hook (first line) + caption (2–4 sentences max) + CTA
- YouTube Short: Title + description
- Standalone caption: works on Instagram feed or LinkedIn

### Day 10 — Teaching Post

## WEEK 3 — Specific Use Cases + Deep Dives

### Day 15 — The SOPs Post
**Platform:** TikTok / Reels
**Hook:** "Every time you explain the same thing twice, you're losing money."
**Caption:** I asked Claude to document how I onboard a new client. Step by step, who does what, what tools, what the output should look like. Took 20 minutes. Now it's written down. I send it to anyone new who works with me. No more explaining the same thing three times. This is what SOPs are — and AI can write them from a 5-minute conversation.

## WEEK 4 — Systems + Going Deeper

### Day 22 — Content Repurposing
**Platform:** TikTok / Reels
**Hook:** "One idea. Five pieces of content. 30 minutes."
**Caption:** Write one piece of content — an insight, a story, a tip. Then ask Claude to turn it into a TikTok hook, an Instagram caption, a LinkedIn post, a Twitter thread, and a YouTube short description. You'll get different formats, different angles, same core idea. This is how you stay consistent without burning out.

---

### Day 23 — Teaching Post
**Platform:** Instagram / LinkedIn
**Caption:** The fastest way to get better at prompting: paste a prompt you've been using and ask Claude to improve it. Tell it what you were trying to achieve. It'll tell you what's weak, what context is missing, and give you an upgraded version. Then test it. This is how you get better without reading a course or watching tutorials.

---

### Day 24 — Cost Breakdown Deep Dive
**Platform:** TikTok / Reels
**Hook:** "Most tradies don't actually know what they're making per job."
**Caption:** Materials is easy. But what about your overhead per hour? Your unpaid admin time? Your vehicle costs broken down per day? Your slow period buffer? I built a full cost model for every job type I quote. It took one afternoon. Now I know my actual margin before I send anything. If you're quessing, you're leaking money.

---

### Day 25 — Teaching Post
**Platform:** TikTok / Instagram
**Hook:** "Ask AI to play devil's advocate. Thank me later."
**Caption:** Before you commit to any plan, quote, or decision: "Here's what I'm planning to do. Argue against it as hard as you can. What could go wrong? What would a skeptic say?" This is the fastest way to find holes before they cost you. I use this before every big business decision.
